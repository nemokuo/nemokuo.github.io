Fourhandsprint Studio 四手印刷

位於台灣高雄，駁二藝術特區大義倉庫旁巷弄，空間隱身於二樓，致力於印製手感溫度之創作，串聯各式後加工。除了彩色印刷，店內也搭配使用Risograph數位印刷機，提供印製各項平面作品，包含海報、卡片、藝術畫、雜誌禮盒等項目。Risograph目前備有17種油墨顏色疊印選擇，陸續將增加更多顏色選項。除了紙張印刷，工作室也結合傳統網版印刷技法，印製布類週邊，並不定期開設製版教學工作坊，分享傳統製版工藝體驗樂趣。

Our studio is located in Kaohsiung, Taiwan, and specialized in risograph printing. We have a Risograph press, and offer services for printing graphic design works, posters, cards, art prints, zine and more. Currently, our studio has a total of 17 colors of ink to choose from. There will be more ink color options to choose from in the near future. In addition to risograph printing, our studio also offers screen printing. If you need risograph printing or screen printing services, Please feel free to contact us anytime.
